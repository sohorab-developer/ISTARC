                        Background of Science Festival
                        
12 TH ISTARC SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY FESTIVAL 2024

                        
Science festivals are organized over the years to discover the latent talent inside the young generation of our country. To give the students a different vibe of education, it is very important. Science festival helps a student
                            to do something by his/her own creativity. Students from different classes participate in this event to express their innovative ideas and views. It helps us to create a strong platform for the young scientists of our country.
                            And many of the innovative projects are also in various welfare of our countries. So, for the betterment of our country and for the betterment of our students, science fair is very necessary. There are various Olympiads in
                            science fair. And Olympiads make students to entitle their lucid performance and to show their potential in particular discipline of science.
                        

                    

                    


                        About Our Science Festival
                        
Science fairs are arranged in our country for many years. And for the success of this event, it is getting positive recognitions and achieving a higher position. So, to enlighten the nation with amusing science projects and new
                            ideas to solve our problems, it is indeed the best way to accept the ideas from the young talents. And Science festival can do it in the righteous way
